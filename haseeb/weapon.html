H_S WEAPONS

A weapon is any object, tool, or device used to cause harm, damage, or destruction to a person, animal, or thing, often in combat, self-defense, or as part of a conflict. Weapons can take various forms, including physical items like guns, knives, and bombs, or even more abstract forms such as cyber tools used in digital warfare. They are designed for offense or defense and can be classified into several categories, such as:

Melee Weapons: Used in close combat, such as swords, axes, or clubs. Projectile Weapons: Fire projectiles, like bows, guns, and slingshots. Explosives: Devices that cause damage through explosions, such as grenades or landmines. Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear Weapons (CBRN): Weapons that cause destruction through chemicals, biological agents, or nuclear reactions. Energy Weapons: Use directed energy to inflict harm, such as lasers.
